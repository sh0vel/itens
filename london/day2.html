🚗 Day 2 – Tuesday, August 26
Cotswolds Day 1 (Self-Drive)

Activities Time
🚗 Depart London for the Cotswolds
  • Pick up rental car from Kensington or Victoria around 7:30 AM
  • Drive ~2.5 hrs with light traffic
 Morning
🏘️ Arrive in Bibury
  • Visit Arlington Row
  • Stroll the River Coln
  • Optional tea stop at The Swan Hotel
 Late Morning
🏞️ Explore Bourton-on-the-Water
  • Riverfront walk, footbridges, independent shops
  • Option to visit the Model Village or Motoring Museum
 Early Afternoon
🍽️ Lunch at The Croft or Smiths of Bourton Early Afternoon
🚶 Walk or short drive to Lower Slaughter
  • Peaceful village with a streamside walk and old mill
  • One of the most romantic villages in England
 Afternoon
🏘️ Arrive in Stow-on-the-Wold
  • Explore market square, bookstores, antique shops
  • Find Tolkien’s “Doors of Durin” at St. Edward’s Church
 Late Afternoon
🛏️ Check into inn or B&B (e.g., The Porch House or The Old Stocks Inn) Late Afternoon
🍷 Dinner at a local pub – try The Bell at Stow or The Sheep on Sheep Street
  • Cozy atmosphere, seasonal menus
 Evening
