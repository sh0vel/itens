🖼️ Day 4 – Thursday, August 28: Art, Architecture, Shopping & a Late Night Out

Activities Time
☕ Light morning start + exploration
  • Grab coffee at Brill Café near Exmouth Market (vinyl + espresso)
  • Explore Exmouth Market + Leather Lane Market (local, creative stalls and food)
 Morning
🖼️ Stop by The Photographers’ Gallery (Soho) or Whitechapel Gallery (East London)
  • Compact and well-curated, great for ~45-minute visit
 Late Morning
🛍️ Carnaby Street, Liberty London, and Selfridges
  • Fashion, concept stores, and London retail energy
  • Pick up cologne, sunglasses, or UK brands you can’t get in the US
 Early Afternoon
🍝 Lunch at Padella (near Borough Market)
  • Handmade pasta, must arrive around 11:30 AM to avoid lines
  • Signature dishes: pici cacio e pepe, tagliarini with crab
 Early Afternoon
🖼️ Visit Tate Britain (quieter than Tate Modern)
  • See Turner, Blake, and more in a classic setting
  • Optional: walk over to V&A Museum or Wellcome Collection if you're still energized
 Afternoon
🌇 Sunset at Sky Garden (book ahead for 5:30–6:00 PM slot)
  • Lush indoor garden with panoramic views
  • Free entry with reservation — cocktails available at the bar
 Early Evening
🍽️ Dinner at Bob Bob Ricard Soho or Brigadiers (rich, stylish, and bold)
  • BBR: “Press for Champagne” button, Russian-British luxe comfort food
  • Brigadiers: Indian barbecue & whisky, luxe clubby feel
 Evening
🍸 Rooftop pregame
  • Aviary Rooftop (slick, high-rise city views)
  • Or Sabine Rooftop Bar (glasshouse garden style near St. Paul’s)
 Night
🎧 Late-night club (Deep/House/Electronic)
  • Phonox (Brixton) – top-tier sound system, curated house nights
  • XOYO (Shoreditch) – more diverse but solid electronic nights
  • NT’s Loft (Dalston) – loft-style venue with underground vibes
 Late Night
