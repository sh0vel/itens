🇬🇧 Day 1 – Monday, August 25: Arrive from Dublin + Mayfair Evening

Activities Time
✈️ Flight from Dublin → London Heathrow
  • Land at 1:00 PM
  • Domestic arrival – faster baggage claim and exit
  • Take a taxi or Heathrow Express into central London (~45–60 min)
 Early Afternoon
🏨 Hotel check-in and short rest
  • Arrive at hotel by ~2:30–3:00 PM
  • Unpack, shower, relax
 Mid Afternoon
☕ Optional café stop if you want to explore pre-dinner:
  • Everbean – tucked-away, cozy spot for great coffee and pastries
  • Hideaway – elegant espresso bar near Green Park
  • Mount St. Restaurant Café – upscale café near Grosvenor Square
 Late Afternoon
🚶 Optional stroll near hotel:
  • Berkeley Square – quiet, leafy Mayfair square
  • New Bond Street – window shop or browse British boutiques
  • Grosvenor Square – stately U.S.-inspired park in central Mayfair
 Late Afternoon
🍽️ Dinner at Gymkhana (Mayfair)
  • Reservation at 7:00 PM recommended
  • Must-try: wild muntjac biryani, tandoori masala lamb chops
  • Sophisticated interiors, colonial club–style atmosphere
 Evening
🍸 Optional nightcap:
  • SOMA Soho – minimalist, moody cocktails with subtle Indian influence
  • Swift Soho – excellent upstairs/downstairs cocktail experience
  • Claridge’s Bar – refined, luxury cocktails if you want to end on a high note
 Evening
